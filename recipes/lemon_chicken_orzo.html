One Pot Lemon Chicken Orzo

Back to Home

Description

A super simple and homey pasta recipe. Quick and easy, this recipes easily scales to feed more, and conveniently uses just one pot!

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup chopped yellow onion
  • 1 tbsp chopped garlic
  • 1 cup uncooked orzo
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 2 lemons, zested and juiced
  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 3 cups baby spinach
  • 1/2 cup shredded fontina cheese
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 and 1/2 cups of chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
  • 3/4 tbsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Heat oil in a large Dutch oven on medium. Add onion and cook, stirring occasionally, until softened, 2 to 3 minutes. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds. Stir in orzo and cook until lightly toasted, about 1 minute
  3. Stir in heavy cream, broth, about 1 tbsp lemon zest,m and 3 tbsp of lemon juice. bring to a simmer on medium heat, stirring occasionally, about 3 minutes. Reduce heat to medium-low. Simmer, stirring occasionally, about 8 to 10 minutes.
  4. Stir in chicken and spinach until spinach wilts, about 1 minute. Remove from heat. Stir in Fontina, 1/4 cup of Parmesan, 2 cups of parsley, pepper, and salt about 30 seconds
  5. Serve immediately, topped with Parmesan and parsley

Enjoy!
