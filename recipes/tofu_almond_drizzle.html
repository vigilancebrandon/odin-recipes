Tofu Almond Drizzle

Back to Home

Description

Tofu almond drizzle is a delicious dish, and makes a hearty and simple meal for the whole family.

With a rice of your choice as the base, enjoy a juicy and enriching kale side, topped with marinated tofu and a flavorful almond-sesame sauce

Ingredients

Marinated Tofu

  • 3 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 4 to 5 slices (1/8") ginger
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tbsp brown-rice vinegar
  • 3 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1/3 cup tamari
  • 1 lb. firm tofu

Base

  • 1 large bunch kale, rinsed and chopped
  • 1 large pot of water
  • 1 pinch fo salt (optional)
  • 2 cups of rice (brown or jasmine)

Tofu

  • 1/4 cup creamy almond butter
  • 2 tsp maple syrup
  • 2 tbsp tamari
  • 1 tbsp brown rice vinegar
  • tbsp grated ginger
  • 1 to 2 tsp hot pepper oil
  • 1/3 cup of water

Steps

Tofu

  1. In the morning, combine garlic, ginger, water, vinegar, oil, and tamari for the marinade
  2. cut tofu into 1/2-inch slabs, then slabs into triangles
  3. Place marinade and tofu in a glass container for 30 minutes to 8 hours, more time equals stronger flavor
  4. Heat a lightly oiled skillet on medium-high
  5. Place the tofu in the pan and brown on both sides. Fry in batches if necessary. You may also opt to bake in the oven!

Base

  1. Cook the rice in a medium pot (depending on your rice choice, instructions may be different, so we are leaving it to you!)
  2. Bring large pot of (opt. salted) water to a boil
  3. Submerge kale and boil for 5 to 8 minutes
  4. Pour cooked kale into a colander in the sink and let cool

Sauce

  1. Mix all ingredients in a mixing bowl
  2. Place sauce in a medium sauce pan on medium heat
  3. Stir as it warms, letting it gradually thicken
  4. Add water for desired consistency
  5. Plate, and drizzle sauce over all

Enjoy!
